About

TreeTalky AI/VR is an immersive Virtual Reality (VR) application that combines the power of LLM-based conversational artificial intelligence (AI) with immersive VR to create a learning environment in which learners can talk to a virtual tree from the Amazon rainforest.

The application has been developed by Pia Spangenberger and Felix Reuth as part of their research in the field of technology-enhanced learning and teaching.

Game Design Studio The Good Evil was providing technical support designing the final version.

Biographies

Pia Spangenberger

Pia Spangenberger’s, PhD, conducts research on technology-enhanced learning and teaching, with an emphasis on empirically investigating cognitive, affective, and behavioral learning outcomes in the context of Education for Sustainable Development (ESD). Her work explores interactive and immersive media, including immersive Virtual Reality (VR), Serious Games, and GenAI-based conversational agents.

Georg Felix Reuth

Georg Felix Reuth, PhD candidate, is a specialist in generative AI for learning. He has engineered and deployed LLM solutions for numerous domestic and global educational research projects over a four-year period.
